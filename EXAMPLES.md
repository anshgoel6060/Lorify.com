## 💡 Tips for Best Results

### For Roast Mode:
1. **Be specific** - More details = better roasts
2. **Include context** - Explain the situation fully
3. **Try different personalities** - Each has unique humor
4. **Keep it real** - Authentic situations get better responses

### For Court Mode:
1. **Provide both sides** - Give full context
2. **Include specific examples** - "He did X when I did Y"
3. **Mention patterns** - "This happens every time..."
4. **Be honest** - The AI can detect when you're hiding details

## 🚫 What NOT to Input

### Avoid:
- ❌ Hate speech or discrimination
- ❌ Threats or violence
- ❌ Personal information (phone numbers, addresses)
- ❌ Illegal activities
- ❌ Extremely sensitive topics without context

### Instead:
- ✅ Funny life situations
- ✅ Relationship drama (anonymized)
- ✅ Career/life choices
- ✅ Social media fails
- ✅ Everyday struggles
